Overview

Welcome to the northern Finland! We like to call this place Winter Wonderland, a fun and relaxing place where you and your family can have as much fun as you like in the snow. You will make unforgettable memories in this magical place all alone.

From wandering the forest to gliding across the frozen lakes, there's something for everyone. Whether you're here for the peaceful beauty of nature or the friendly neighbours. Come have fun today!
